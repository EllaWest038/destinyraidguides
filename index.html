Welcome to Destiny Raid Guides! We store guides on all raids currently in the world of Destiny 2, and keep records of all those that have been placed into the Destiny Content Vault over the years.

Below you can find more information on the different types of content we store.


Active Raids

Active raids are ones that are currently available to play in Destiny 2. As of Season of the Haunted, there are five active raids in Destiny 2 with another planned to return from Destiny 1 in Season 18.

The currently of the five active raids, four originated in Destiny 2 with the yearly expansions. The fifth raid was revamped from Destiny 1 and released during Season 14.

View Raids

Vaulted Raids

Vaulted raids are those that are unavailable to play in Destiny 2 but may return to the game further down the track. There are two different types of raids in this category - ones from Destiny 1 and ones from Destiny 2.

Everything from Destiny 1, that hasn't already been moved over into Destiny 2, is counted as being vaulted content. Despite this, all Destiny 1 raids are still available to play in the original game.

When the Beyond Light expansion launched for Destiny 2 in early November 2020, Bungie placed five of the game's raids into the Destiny Content Vault as part of a massive system update for the game. These raids are no longer available to play on any platform, but may return in the future.
