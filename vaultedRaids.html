Vaulted Raids

  • Home
  • Vaulted Raids

Vaulted raids are ones that are unavailable to play in Destiny 2 but may return to the game further down the track. There are two different types of raids in this category - ones from Destiny 1 and ones from Destiny 2.


Destiny 1

Everything from Destiny 1, that hasn't already been moved over into Destiny 2, is counted as being vaulted content. Despite this, all Destiny 1 raids are still available to play in the original game.

View Raids

Destiny 2

When the Beyond Light expansion launched for Destiny 2 in early November 2020, Bungie placed five of the game's raids into the Destiny Content Vault as part of a massive system update for the game. These raids are no longer available to play on any platform, but may return in the future.
